"Create a whimsical, educational adventure video game concept and key visuals in the style of 'The Magic School Bus' meets '2001: A Space Odyssey.'
The game is called 'Magic Space Bus: Trumpovirus Odyssey'.
Core premise: Ms. Frizzle and her class shrink down inside a magical, shape-shifting school bus that can travel through outer space and microscopic worlds. Their mission takes them into the mysterious Fox Constellation — a glowing, fox-shaped cluster of stars and nebulae that hides an ancient alien monolith. Inside this constellation, they discover that Donald Trump has been personified as a giant, orange, wig-wearing Rhinovirus — a boastful, meme-speaking common cold virus who believes he is destined to 'Make the Microverse Great Again.'
Gameplay blends:

Classic Magic School Bus educational exploration (learn real science about viruses, immune systems, space travel, evolution, and AI).
Epic 2001: A Space Odyssey vibes with slow-zoom starfields, mysterious black monoliths that trigger evolutionary leaps, a rogue ship AI named 'HAL 2024' that sings show tunes and occasionally malfunctions with conspiracy theories.
Humorous, non-partisan absurdity: Trumpovirus floats around with tiny golden tie tendrils, rallies other viruses with speeches ('We're gonna build a wall around the nucleus!'), and causes chaotic 'infections' that players must navigate or cure.

Key features:

The Magic School Bus transforms into a glowing spaceship, then into antibodies, then into a microscopic submarine.
Levels alternate between vast psychedelic space sequences (Jupiter-like gas giants made of viral proteins, star gates, the 'Star Child' evolution scene reimagined with cartoon cells) and inner-body adventures inside a giant cosmic fox host.
Puzzle-solving, collection of 'knowledge orbs,' boss battles against Trumpovirus who grows bigger by 'spreading' misinformation particles.
Educational pop-ups and Frizzle facts about rhinoviruses, constellations, DNA, and human space exploration.
Visual style: Vibrant 90s cartoon aesthetic mixed with Kubrick-inspired symmetrical space shots, glowing nebulae, and playful character designs. Trumpovirus has a big glowing orange capsid head, tiny hands, and constantly tweets tiny floating text bubbles.

Tone: Fun, educational, surreal, and slightly psychedelic — perfect for all ages but with clever adult references to 2001 and modern culture. Include concept art descriptions for title screen, gameplay screenshots, character models, and box art."
